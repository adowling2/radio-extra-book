\chapter*{Exam Scope and Independence Note}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Exam Scope and Independence Note}
This book is an independent educational study guide. It is not affiliated with,
endorsed by, or sponsored by ARRL, NCVEC, or the FCC.

The supporting reference files include older and historical question-pool study
guides. For that reason, this book avoids claiming that any specific question
identifier is current. The durable circuit theory remains useful, but final exam
preparation should be checked against the official current Amateur Extra
question pool and the current FCC rules.
